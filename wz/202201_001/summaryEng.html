Regional Difference and Polarization Trends of Financial Inclusion in China: 2005-2016

  LIN Chun  KANG Kuan  SUN Ying-jie

Abstract：Based on the connotation of financial inclusion development, this paper improves the Chinese provincial evaluation system of financial inclusion and then measures. Based on this, The Gini coefficient and DER index are used to measure the regional difference and polarization trends under three and eight comprehensive regions respectively, and the above indexes are decomposed. The results show that the regional difference and polarization degree of financial inclusion development in China show a downward trend, and there is heterogeneity in the evolution trend of different regions. The growth rate of difference is relatively low in the regions with greater difference in financial inclusion development, that is, the degree of difference in the development of different regions has the tendency of convergence; The growth rate of polarization is relatively low in the regions with greater polarization in financial inclusion development, that is, the degree of difference in the development of different regions has the tendency of convergence; the difference between regions is the main source of the overall difference; the changing trend of DER index is caused by the changing trend of alienation.

Key words：Financial Inclusion，Regional Difference， Gini Coefficient，DER Index
